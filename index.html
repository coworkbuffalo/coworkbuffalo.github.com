cowork buffalo

Work + Community. Can't get more Buffalo than that.
Working at home, out of coffee shops, or in Buffalo for the day? Coworking is for you.

Get some directions.

info

You can find us at 653 Main Street (Suite 104), directly across from Shea's Performing Arts Center, 10am-6pm on weekdays.

We'll have delicious snacks, reliable & fast wifi, and an open desk space waiting for you.

Our desk space is a mix of social, open tables and heads-down desks in the center of the city.

plans

Our prices for individuals are simple, because we want to get smart Buffalonians and visitors together. $125/month, $15/day, or $125 for a 10-day Flex Pass, good for six months.

We take cash or credit cards via Square, and offer monthly billing, too, with receipts for everyone.

Are you a small company, development team, or other group looking for a flexible space? Contact us about team discounts.

space

We want you to love this office. Many of our current members are based in technology fields. We're open to all professions that need to cowork.

We're also open to host your event, meetup, or group at the space! If you'd like to reserve our space for your team, a meeting with clients, or otherwise, check our calendar for availability. Follow us at @CoworkBuffalo to see what's going on recently at the space!
