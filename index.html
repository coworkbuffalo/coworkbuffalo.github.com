cowork buffalo

Work + Community. Can't get more Buffalo than that.
Working at home, out of coffee shops, or in Buffalo for the day? Coworking is for you.

Get some directions.

info

You can find us at 225 Delaware Avenue (Suite 10), above Spot Coffee, 10am-6pm on weekdays.

We'll have delicious snacks, reliable & fast wifi, and an open desk space waiting for you.

Our desk space is a mix of social, open tables and heads-down desks with great views of the city.

plans

Our prices for individuals are simple, because we want to get smart Buffalonians and visitors together. $100/month, $10/day, or $75 for a 10-day Flex Pass, which you can use any time.

We take cash or credit cards via Square, and offer monthly billing, too, with receipts for everyone.

Are you a small company, development team, or other group looking for a flexible space? Contact us about team discounts.

space

We want you to love this office. Many of our current members are based in technology fields. We're open to all professions that need to cowork.

We're also open to host your event, meetup, or group at the space! Follow us at @CoworkBuffalo to see what's going on now, and check out NextPlex for a calendar of events here in the city.
